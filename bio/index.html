    • Life & Interests

    As a child and a teenager, I saw myself as an unfitting member of society for my lack of capacity to interact with people around me, but this is no sob story. As time went by I was able to accept myself for who I am, which led me to try my best to achieve solitude.

    Throughout this little journey of self-discovery, I've noticed how learning things gives me a sense of reason in life. Some of the many things I've learned over the years were music production, focusing on sound-design-oriented music genres, photography, all sorts of instruments but mainly the electric guitar, vector-based 2d animation, and more recently, programming.

    "There is something so mesmerizing about the apparent stillness of nature. The song 'motion in emotional hiatus' tries to incorporate that feeling through subtle changes in melodies, textures, and constant progression, representing nature's continuous growth and it's life cycle."
